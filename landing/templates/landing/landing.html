Integrative or Completmently Healthcare

Integrative health is an individualized, client-centered model or promoting optimal health and wellness.

Integrative health also emphasizes multimodal interventions, which are two or more interventions such as conventional medicine, lifestyle modification, physical rehabilitation, psychotherapy, and complementary health approaches in various combinations with an emphasis on treating the whole person rather than, for example, targeting one organ system. Integrative health aims for well coordinate care among different providers and institutions by bringing conventional and complementary approaches together to care for the whole person.

According to National Center for Completmently and Integrative Health
